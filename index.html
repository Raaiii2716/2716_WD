About Our Resort

Nestled in the stunning landscape of Portugal's Algarve region, Hole In One offers a world-class golfing experience on our championship course designed by renowned architect Robert Trent Jones Jr.

Our resort combines challenging golf with luxurious accommodation, exceptional dining, and premium facilities to create the perfect golf getaway. The course features dramatic elevation changes, strategically placed water hazards, and breathtaking ocean views from several holes.

With over 300 days of sunshine annually, the Algarve is Europe's premier golf destination. Our resort has received the "Portugal's Best Golf Resort" award for three consecutive years and was recently ranked among the top 50 golf resorts worldwide.
